<i>Package Manager</i> is a highly powerful application to manage apps, both system and user, installed on an android device.

<b>WARNING: I Am NOT Responsible for any Damages on Your Device!</b>

<b>ROOT</b> Access is required for some advanced features.

<b>Features</b>

<i>Package Manager</i> is a simple, yet powerful application, capable to do the following things</b>
* Offer a beautiful list view of System and User Apps, together or separately.
* Helps to do basic tasks such as Open app, show App info, visit PlayStore page, uninstall (User apps), etc.
* Do advanced tasks such as (need Root access)
  * Uninstall an individual or a batch of system apps (de-bloating)
  * Export (to sdcard) and Share an app
  * Export Split apk's to sdcard
  * Install Split apk's from sdcard
  * Disable or Enable a batch of apps together
  * Back up an individual or a batch of app data
  * Restore individual app data
